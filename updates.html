Project Updates

Track the progress of the 9.4T MRI development and installation.

JAN 2026

Website Launch

The official project website has been launched to keep the scientific community and the public informed about the progress of the 9.4T Human MRI scanner at NKI. We will be posting regular updates here.

2025

Lab Facility Construction

Construction of the UHF lab facility to house the 9.4T system is underway. New York State is supporting the estimated $5m budget for this custom laboratory, which includes specific shielding and structural requirements for the magnet.

2024

Magnet Acquisition & Testing

NKI purchased the 9.4T magnet from the University of Minnesota. The magnet has been transported to temporary storage in Kearny, NJ. Its integrity has been independently certified by Tesla Engineeringâ€™s magnet division.

2024

Partnership with GE Global Research

GE Global Research has entered a partnership with NKI to co-develop the 9.4T system. They will be providing the new HG4 head-only gradient coil, a critical component for achieving high-resolution imaging.
